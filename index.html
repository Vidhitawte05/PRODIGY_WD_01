About Trust

The Prodigy Infotech Trust commonly known as Prodigy Infotech Educate in the educational circles is considered as one of the largest educational trusts in Maharashtra. This trust is 94 years old. The first batch was started in 1912 under the name of Prodigy Infotech Educare by a handful of dedicated social workers. These founding fathers believed in the spread of education as the necessary ingredient needed for achieving freedom from ignorance.
